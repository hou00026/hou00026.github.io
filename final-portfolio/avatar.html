Avatar Design

Student Project - 2020

In an effort to design an avatar, I cared deeply about the difference between the various character classes, whether a ninja, tiger, or cat. Ninja needs to be loyal, valiant, practice hard, fit into the environment, stay calm with a lot of patience. I think it's treasurable character that I loved. So, I created it as my logo for the motivating and encouraging in the future self-driven studies.

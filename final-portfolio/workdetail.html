Bonsai Website Redesign

Student Project

I redesigned home page and the process of shopping steps. To improve the user experience, solve the problems that the original website has. I need to identify the problems and figure out how could I enhance this website to make it more attrative and easy for shopping?

First, based on the feedbacks of my friend, the mainly problem of original website is there were massive informations that will be distract users and the promotions was too much which lead meaningful engagement was very low.

Second, a lot of categories were placed in the navigation bar, which resulted in visually clutter.

Solutions :
I was expecting to improved it visually and functionally. I redesigned a new logo to make it more adaptable to different devices and reorganized the navigation bar, as well as reduced promotions to two as the primary engagement area to accommodate the marketing needs.

Delivarables

Visual branding
UI & UX design

Years

2020

Objective

Redesign a website to showcase the products, refresh uses' eye. Increasing the traffic and reducing the bounce rate as well.

Compare - Preview Browse and preview at the same time

The biggest difference between the old and new design is the visual effect and the organization of the layout.

STEP - 1 Research & Define basic idea

Did marketing research, analyzed the target audiences and figured out the resolutions. A brand new basic idea came out. A clean, flat, simplicity style brand identity was designed.

STEP - 2 Wireframe

After defined the idea and sitemap, it's time to wireframe and prototype the user flow diagram.

STEP - 3 Visualized

Defined visual rules to limite clutter,fine-tuning all elements with consistency style.

STEP - 4 Make it responsive

Make the website responsive to adapt different devices for user easy to read.
